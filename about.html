About Ray Town

Luxury, Heritage, and Exceptional Hospitality

Our Story

1984 - Founded

Ray Town Hotel was founded by Ravi Yadav with a vision to bring royal Indian hospitality to every guest. Starting with just 12 rooms, the journey of excellence began.

1990 - Expansion

With growing popularity, the hotel expanded its rooms and added a fine-dining restaurant offering authentic Indian cuisine.

2000 - Luxury Suites

Introduced the first set of luxury suites, blending contemporary comfort with traditional Indian architecture and decor.

2010 - Wellness & Spa

Opened the award-winning spa and wellness center, providing holistic relaxation experiences for all guests.

2020 - Global Recognition

Ray Town became internationally recognized for exceptional hospitality, hosting dignitaries and travelers worldwide.

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

To provide luxury hospitality with personalized care, blending Indian tradition and modern comfort.

Vision

To be recognized globally as the epitome of Indian luxury hotels while promoting culture and elegance.

Values

Integrity, Excellence, Respect, Warmth, and Commitment to guest satisfaction.
